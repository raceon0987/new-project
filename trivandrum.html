Trivandrum

Thiruvananthapuram also known as Trvandrum, the capital of Gods Own Country is famous for its beaches and resorts. Asia's largest mall is situatuted there. Even though the city commands rush vibe there is much more to see in Trivandrum. From Temples Rich in its heritage , Zoological park and museums to trekking in the forests and water sports in the sea it offers wide variety of attractions to tourists. 'Attukal Ponkal' a religious festival which sees participation over many thousands people is annually held there. Sree Padmanabha Swamy Temple and adjuscent Palace tells the story of revelutionary king of Trivandrum . The hill station Ponmudy offers a scenic beauty of nature which attracts tourists all year round
