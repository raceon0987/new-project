Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known popularly as the 'City of Spices' and the 'City of Sculptures' offer a lush green landscape, tranquil beaches, backwater and a number of historic sites. The central Kozhikode Beach, overlooked by an old lighthouse, is a popular spot for watching the sunset. Inland, tree-lined Mananchira Square, with its musical fountain, surrounds the massive Mananchira Tank, an artificial pond The month of January is the best time to visit as annual literature fest take place during same period whic gives Kozhikode a vibrant colour.
